Announcement

Heading

Singapore based food delivery platform Deliver.sg, owned by Maxima Logistics Pte Ltd, has made a significant move in its ambitious expansion strategy by acquiring Indian Mobillity Lifestyle Platform BusyBee.
Since its launch in 2019 by Krishnamani Kannan and Naresh Kumar Rajendran, Deliver.sg has grown from filling a gap in the long-distance food delivery sector to becoming a trusted provider in the mobility ecosystem. Offering services from food delivery to MAAS (Mobillity as a Service) for corporate and government entities, the platform has carved a robust online presence, all while maintaining a positive EBITDA.
Now, with an aggressive growth plan, Deliver.sg is spreading its wings to Asia and the Middle East via joint ventures and acquisitions
